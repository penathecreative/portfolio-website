About

Olá! I'm Bruno, a passionate full-stack web developer currently based in the Netherlands, though my journey started in Portugal. While I hold a bachelor’s degree in International Relations, my professional path took me through 4 years of customer support, where I managed teams and learned the ins and outs of working with diverse clients and teams. That experience taught me leadership, problem-solving, and adaptability—skills I now bring into the world of tech. I've always had a love for computers, fueled by my early years as a gamer, and that passion led me to pursue web development. Whether it’s designing an intuitive user interface or building robust back-end systems, I’m constantly excited by the challenges and creative possibilities in technology. Combining my background in team management and my technical expertise, I’m eager to build digital experiences that make an impact while bringing a fresh perspective rooted in real-world interaction.
